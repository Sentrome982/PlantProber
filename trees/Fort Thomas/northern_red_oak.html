Shumard Oak
Quercus shumardii

The Shumard oak (Quercus shumardii) is a magnificent deciduous tree native to the southern United States. It can reach impressive heights of up to 80 feet, boasts glossy green leaves that turn vibrant orange-red in fall, and provides valuable acorns for wildlife.
