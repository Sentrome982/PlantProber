American Sycamore
Platanus occidentalis

The American Sycamore is known for its unique, peeling bark that reveals patches of white, gray, and brown underneath. In the fall, its broad leaves turn a golden yellow, and it produces round seed balls that hang from its branches. Growing up to 100 feet tall, it thrives near rivers and streams, providing essential habitat for wildlife!
