Yellow Buckeye
Aesculus flava

The Yellow Buckeye blooms with yellow flowers in spring and produces large, shiny seeds that resemble deer eyes. In the fall, its leaves turn a stunning orange. It grows well in moist, well-drained soils and can reach up to 80 feet tall, providing great shade. The tree's wood is soft, making it popular for crafting lightweight items like furniture and toys!
