Plant Prober - Bradford Pear

The Callery pear is an ornamental pear tree native to Asia which has become an invasive plant. It was introduced to North America in the early 1900s for agricultural use and quickly became a favorite in landscaping for their adaptability, flowering, fall color, and rounded crown.
